    What is this?

    As more people join and type, the big square to the left will fill up with what looks like gibberish.

    The User List above this has the usernames of all the people typing, or that joined after you. Click one of them to see their text extracted from the gibberish and placed in the 'User Text' area to the left.

    This is a work in progress, and just for fun!

    github repo here

    This demo uses node, socket.io, less CSS, gulp, jquery, and jasmine for unit testing.
